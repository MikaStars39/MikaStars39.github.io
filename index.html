About Me
Hi there! My name is Qingyu Yin, and I am an undergraduate student from Zhejiang University, class of 2022. I am currently pursuing a dual degree in English and Computer Science. At present, I am fortunate to be interning at the HIC AI Lab with Prof. Qiang Zhang as my tutor. I'm also collaborating with Prof. Xiaoyu Shen from East Institute of Technology, Ningbo.

My research interests lie at (1) advanced architectures designed for future LLM, as well as (2) utilizing machine learning system techniques (hardware-efficient algorithm, cuda kernels, etc.) to advance scientific research in the field of LLM architecture . My work is dedicated to discovering more efficient LLM infrastructure and developing a universal framework suitable for various downstream task scenarios, while exploring the interpretability of these models.

I also focus on genomics and transcriptomics, and am exploring how to use AI tools to accelerate scientific discoveries in related fields.
